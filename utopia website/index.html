A note from the founders
ONWARD AND FORWARD FOREVER
Ever since the inception of the idea of Utopia, there has been eager anticipation and curiosity about what fuels this exclusive society at the heart of technology and innovation.
What began as a shared vision brought together a dedicated team: Harshvardhan Singh Bisht (B.Tech CSE 2021–25), Vedansh Goel (B.Tech CSE 2022–26), Shikhar Rai (B.Tech CSE 2022–26), and Tejasv Kohli (B.Tech CSE 2022–26). Together, we built Utopia as a beacon of quality, effort, leadership, and exclusivity.
These values are not just ideals — they are the foundation of everything we do. We remain committed to upholding them in every step we take and every initiative we lead. As Utopia continues to evolve, these principles will remain at its core, guiding us toward a future shaped by innovation and driven by purpose.

— The Founders of Utopia
